Artiseum, The Social-interactive Web Art Gallery

As the number of artists increases, publishing their works has become a difficult task. Since it takes a lot of money to release their work, many of them face the limitation and give up their art easily.

By increasing use of smart device, people spend a lot of time with social media in their daily lives. A lot of people want to share their life through their image and video, and indeed the impact of social media is enormous.

we are going to improve this problem as the website that can publish their art using social-interactive model, for the nameless artists. Therefore, artists can publish their art more easily and can communicate with people.
